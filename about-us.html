About Us

A Typical Journey to D2 is a short, comedic film that pokes fun at the everyday drama of dining at NYUAD's famous D2 hall. What began as a casual idea quickly grew into a playful story that every student here can relate to.

As students ourselves, we wanted to capture the shared humor and frustrations of campus life. From the scorching walk over to the stubborn doors, we've lived these moments just like everyone else, and turning them into comedy felt like the perfect way to bring that shared experience to life.

The film takes viewers through the highs and (mostly) lows of the D2 experience: battling with doors that never seem to work, waiting in lines that test the limits of human patience, and realizing too late that your meal swipes have run out. It's an exaggerated, funny reflection of a reality all NYUAD students know too well.

More On D2

Since our film unfolds almost entirely within D2, it only makes sense to shine a spotlight on the setting itself. Officially known as the West Dining Hall, D2 houses a mix of restaurants and food counters that try to satisfy the endless cravings of a very hungry campus. Inside, you'll find a variety of counters serving everything from quick bites to full meals, each with its own quirks and fan favorites.
